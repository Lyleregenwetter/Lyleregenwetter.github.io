About Me

Hi! I'm Lyle Regenwetter, an incongruous combination of proud midwesterner, dedicated mountain lover, and aspiring foodie. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at MIT. I grew up in Champaign, Illinois home of the University of Illinois, and over my life my interests have shifted from math competitions in my childhood to robotics in my high school and college years to data-driven design and AI in my graduate studies.

Academic Bio

I received my S.M. in Mechanical Engineering from MIT in 2022 and before that received a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering with highest honors from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2020. I've had the priviledge of several academic distinctions, such as the Chancellor's scholorship, Association for Facilities Engineering Award, and Kenneth J. Trigger award at UIUC and the Morningside Academy of Design Fellowship at MIT.
